I am a freelance set designer, visual display artist, decorator, prop maker and sometimes even a florist. Originally from Warsaw, Poland, but now I can call Australia my home.

I spent the last 5 years in London, United Kingdom, where I gained my degree in Fashion Retail Branding and Visual Merchandising at London College of Fashion. I have worked with such brands as: Anthropologie, The Conran Shop, Embassy of Canada, Hawes and Curtis. I also helped with organizing London Fashion Week in 2014 and in 2015.

I am passionate window designer that can make wonderful installation in your window, in eco-friendly way. My handmade decorations stand out by the quality of craftsmanship and attention to detail. The unconventional materials and original techniques that I use will always be a show stopper, as for me anything can be an inspiration, starting from a paper clip to reclaimed denim jeans. I can offer you comprehensive decorating services with handmade decorations as an experienced designer, I can make anything you can imagine - you name it!
